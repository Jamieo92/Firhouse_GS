Up Coming Golf Outings

Im excited to announce that our first event will take place on the 17thof June 2023.
This will take place in the magnificent Lisheen Springs Golf Club. Be sure to check
out their website and get an idea of the course you will be playing. The tournament
will be stableford rules and all players must have an official handicap from golf Ireland,
if not you will be playing off 18 and will not qualify for the prizes. Entry will be €40 per person.

Macreddin Golf Course

Our next outing will be on the 22ndof July in Macreddin Golf Club. This is one of my favourite golf courses in Ireland with some amazing views and is just a hidden gem. It was designed by Irish proffesinal golfer and Ryder cup captain Paul McGinley so you know this course is going to be spectacular. The price will be €50 per person with soup an sandwithches beforehand and then a prize giving and some drinks afterwards. There is the option to stay overnight aswel so get in contact with myself for more information on that.

Enniscrone Golf Club

Finally we have Ennicrone Golf club on the 26th of August. Enniscrone is a links golf course with some spectacular and runs along Enniscrone beach. For anyone who hasn't played a links course before you are in for a treat but be sure to bring plenty of golf balls. It is sure to be a windy day and will be very challenging but do not miss the oppurtunity to play this one folks. So if you're interested get in contact with myself Jamie O'Neill or just fill out our form on the joining page.
